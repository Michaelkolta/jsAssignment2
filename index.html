Chicken Tikka Masala

Rich and creamy Indian curry with tender chicken pieces

Extended Preparation Time — This recipe requires more than 45 minutes to prepare. Plan accordingly!
  • 600g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala paste
  • 400ml coconut cream
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons ginger, grated
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  1. Marinate chicken in half the yogurt and 1 tablespoon tikka paste for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan, cook marinated chicken until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté onion until soft. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add remaining tikka paste and canned tomatoes. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in coconut cream and remaining yogurt. Add chicken back to the pan.
